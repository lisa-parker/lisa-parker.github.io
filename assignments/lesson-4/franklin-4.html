Fanklin: Weekly Weather Stories

When It Rains, It Pours!

April showers have been extended this year, due to a series of heavy storms rolling in from the Pacific. Citizens of Franklin prepare for the worst, as they hope for the best. Flooding is expected in many low lying areas, but the city of Franklin has sent out a crew of city workers to assist volunteers to set up sand bags in the most vulnerable areas.
