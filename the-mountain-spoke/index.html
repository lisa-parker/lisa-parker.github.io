More About Mountain Spoke

Here at the Mountain Spoke, we offer mountain bicyclers a full range of quality products and services. To begin with, we stock a wide selection of mountain bicycles, which are top rated for quality and performance. From May through September, we offer daily mountain bicycling tours for cyclists of all skill levels. Our popular tours are located in most the scenic areas surrounding Bozeman, Montana. In addition, we sponsor the annual Cycle the Continental Divide event, which draws bicyclers from around the country. To keep your bicycle in top condition, we also offer full service bike repair and maintenance. Our service providers are well trained and use only the finest parts to ensure your bicycle will provide you with top performance in the field.
