Tour Information

From May through September, we offer daily mountain bicycling tours, so it is easy and convenient to jump on your bike and join the fun whenever it suits you. Our daily tours are located in the scenic areas surrounding Bozeman, Montana. We pride ourselves on providing fun and adventurous tours for bikers of all skill levels. Learn more about our tours in the tour descriptions below. In addition, we sponsor our annual Cycle the Continental Divide Event, which runs for 3 days and 2 nights. Bicyclers come from around the country to enjoy the event with family and friends. For more information, please visit our events page.

Mountain View Trail

Cost: *$135/person

Time: 10-12 hours

Location: 16 miles N of Bozeman

Skill Level: Advanced

This challenging mountain trail is packed with adventure and breath taking views. Experienced cyclers rank this tour number one, and for good reason. The trail conditions vary considerably as you make your way up the winding gravel slopes. Rocky climbs at several intervals along the trial present a considerable challenge, but once you make your way past these steep slopes, you are immediately rewarded for your efforts by stunning views mountain views and cool mountain breezes.

Adventure Trail

Cost: *$95/person

Time: 6-7 hours

Location: 7 miles SW of Bozeman

Skill Level: Intermediate+

Adventure Trail is one of our favorite trails here at the Mountain Spoke. It is a solid mid-level adventure tour, which appeals to those who love dirt trails with lots of hills and vales. Much of the trail meanders along the edge of Rouge River. There are several windy hills to traverse, which provide a nice challenge. But the most loved sections of Adventure trail are the great jump off points, which appeal to cyclists who enjoy putting some air between them and the ground.

Mountain Glide Trail

Cost: *$45/adult | $25/child

Time: 3-4 hours

Location: 5 miles E of Bozeman

Skill Level: Beginner+

Those who are looking for a smooth and relaxed mountain bicycling experience often choose to ride the Mountain Glide trail. This tour is especially great for beginning riders as well as for families with children. The trail is fairly level, with only gently sloping rises as you progress along.
