Daily Tours From May Through September

Tour Information

From May through September, we offer daily mountain bicycling tours, so it is easy and convenient to jump on your bike and join the fun whenever it suits you. Our daily tours are located in the scenic areas surrounding Bozeman, Montana. We pride ourselves on providing fun and adventurous tours for bicyclers of all skill levels. Learn more about our tours in the tour descriptions below. In addition, we sponsor our annual Cycle the Continental Divide Event, which runs for 5 days and 4 nights. Bicyclers come from around the country to enjoy this unique event with family and friends. For more information, please visit our events page.

Daily Mountain Trail Tours

Mountain View

Cost: *$135/person

Length: 21 miles

Skill Level: Advanced

Location: Near Big Timber

This challenging mountain trail is packed with adventure and breath taking views. Experienced cyclers rank this tour number one, and for good reason. The trail conditions vary as you make your way up the winding gravel slopes. Rocky climbs along the trail present a considerable challenge, but once you make your way past these slopes, you are immediately rewarded for your efforts by spectacular the mountain views and cool mountain breezes.

Adventure Trail

Cost: *$95/person

Length: 14 miles

Skill Level: Intermediate+

Location: Near Three Forks

Adventure Trail is among our favorites here at the Mountain Spoke. It's a solid mid-level adventure, which appeals to those who love dirt trails and a lot of hills and vales. Much of the trail meanders along the edge of Rue River. There's a long winding hill to traverse, which is a great challenge. But the most popular sections of Adventure trail are the great natural ramps, which appeal to cyclists who enjoy putting a little air between them and the ground.

Mountain Glide

Cost: *$45/adult | $25/child

Length: 8 miles

Skill Level: Beginner+

Location: Outside Gallatin

Bicyclers looking for a smooth and relaxed mountain bicycling tour often choose the Mountain Glide Trail. This tour is especially great for beginners as well as for families with children. The trail is fairly level, with only gently sloping rises as you progress along. The tour stops for lunch at Pinewood Park to enjoy the refreshing shade and lovely mountain and river views.
