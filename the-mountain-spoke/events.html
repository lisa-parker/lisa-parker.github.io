Cycle the Continental Divide!

Annual Tour – July 8-12

272 Miles of Scenic Mountain Views

The Continental Divide Trail

Every summer, The Mountain Spoke proudly sponsors our own annual Cycle the Continental Divide Event, which runs for 5 days and 4 nights. We will be cycling for aproximately 272 miles along the some of the most scenic sections of the Continental Divide Trail (CDT). The CDT is the longest bicycling trail in the world. It stretches 3,100 miles along the spine of the Great Continental Divide from Canada to Mexico and provides access to some of the most wild and scenic places left in the world (continentaldividetrail.org). We will be traveling along the CDT trail starting from Helena and ending in Roosville (see map below). Come join us for the ride of a lifetime on the wild and scenic Continental Divide Trial.

Tour Information and Details

Evening meals and daily snacks are provided along the trail, as well as unlimited water refills. There are two rest stops along the way where food and supplies can be obtained. Please be sure to plan ahead for any additional needs you may have. Temperatures can be very chilly overnight, so please plan accordingly. We have a trained medical technician on staff and first aid supplies available for emergencies. In addition we offer a free trail guide and a suggested supplies list when you register for our Cycle the Continental Divide Event. Please fill out the registration form and we will send your copy to you right away. We'll see you on the trail!

Cost: $445/person

Time: 5 days and 4 nights

Location: Helena, Montana

Skill Level: Intermediate ‐ Advanced
