Cycle the Continental Divide!

Annual Tour – July 8-12

305 Miles of Scenic Mountain Views

The Continental Divide Trail

Every summer, The Mountain Spoke sponsors the annual Cycle our own Continental Divide event, which runs for 5 days and 4 nights. Our tour runs for 305 miles along the some of the most scenic sections of the Continental Divide Trail (CDT). The CDT is the longest bicycling trail in the world. It stretches 3,100 miles along the spine of the Great Continental Divide from Canada to Mexico and provides access to some of the most wild and scenic places left in the world (Continental Divide Trail.org). We will be traveling the trail from Helena to Roosville (see map below). Come join us for the ride of a life-time on the wild and scenic CDT.

Tour Information and Details

Evening meals and daily snacks are provided along the trail, as well as unlimited water refills. There are two rest stops along the way where food and supplies can be obtained. Please be sure to plan ahead for any additional needs you may have. Temperatures can be very chilly overnight so plan accordingly. We have a trained medical technician on staff and first aid supplies on hand for emergencies. In addition we offer a free trail guide and suggested supplies list when you register for our Cycle the Continental Divid tour. Please fill out the registrations form and we will send your copy to you right away. We'll see you on the trail!

Cost: $445/person

Time: 5 days and 4 nights

Location: Helena, Montana
