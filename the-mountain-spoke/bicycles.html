Onto the Triple butted aluminum frame, Orbea has spec’d the Loki H10 with a FOX 34 Float Performance fork with 130mm travel. SUN RINGLE DUROC 27.5 wheels are wrapped in 3 inch wide Maxxis Rekon+ tires. Rounding out the build is a Race Face ride stem, Race Face Aeffect Dropper seatpost and a 760mm wide bar.

Niner IMBA ROS9 Plus

The first trail-bike style, short chainstay, big tire hardtail is all Niner and all fun. Based on our wildly popular long travel hardtail geometry, the ROS 9 Plus delivers miles of shreddy riding. The bike features short chainstays for a playful feel (437mm at 9 o’clock BB position), while accommodating 3.0 tires for the ultimate in traction and plushness. The 29 Plus platform is the most versatile of the big-tire options – great for off season riding and a functional daily driver – and now we’ve made the most fun version of this platform available.

The ROS 9 Plus is made with 4130 ChroMoly, the go-to material for high abuse applications since the early days of off-road bikes. The tubeset offers the durability and ride quality you want for rowdy rides. Complemented by custom forged 142x12 maxle compatible dropouts in the rear, the frame is designed to resist twist under heavy pedal loads and when cornering and descending. The frame also features high quality details such as internal and external dropper routing, removable cable guides and custom forged seat and chainstay bridges.

Ragley Marley 1.0 27.5” Hardtail

Designed as a trail friendly bike with all mountain capabilities, the Marley is created for exploration, adventure and play. It is for people that ride with their saddle up as much as their saddle down. At home on any terrain; from tearing around your local trail centre, snaking down your local singletrack or back country adventures in the middle of nowhere; it will push your skills to new limits and beyond!

Our updated trail geometry has been designed to mimic the characteristics of a modern full suspension bike but obviously without the sag at the rear. This is why our bikes seem slack when static. However once the rider is on the bike, the front suspension will sag, steepening the head angle by the desired amount.

The new Marley should see you climbing up, going along and flying down faster than ever before!
